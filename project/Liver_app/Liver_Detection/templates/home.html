About Our System

This System presents a cutting-edge approach to liver tumor detection and analysis using Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN). By employing deep learning techniques on CT scan images, the system is designed to streamline the tumor detection process, reducing the risk of human error and improving the speed and accuracy of diagnosis.

The system goes beyond just detecting the presence of tumors; it also classifies tumors into different stages, providing crucial information to healthcare professionals for diagnosis and treatment planning. The model's architecture is carefully crafted, incorporating image processing methods, data augmentation, and CNN model training to achieve precise predictions and results.

System aims to make a significant impact on liver tumors diagnosis, providing medical professionals with advanced tools to enhance their diagnostic capabilities.
